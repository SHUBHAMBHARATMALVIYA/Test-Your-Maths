About Us

Test Your Math is a Math quiz website for kids under 16.
There are 3 categories based on there ages namely: 5 to 8, 9 to 12 and 13 to 16.
The main purpose of this website to improve maths of kids and help them practice maths.
This website improves addition, subtraction and multiplication of kids. By solving quiz
of this website everyday for 15 mins helps them to improve their maths.
